What is PaperTown?

Introduction

PaperTown is a free roll and write game for 2 players that you can print and start playing immediately. All you need is a color printer (B/W version comming soon), a smartphone (used for the 'roll' part) and a pen.

Your Task. You have been asked by the city's mayor together with another construction company to renovate her town. The town is alway different, the goals of the mayor change and the special powers you can collect are different as well. Can you still win?

Procedural levels. While this game is inspired by recent roll and write games such as Noch mal! and board games such as Kingdom Builder, it brings random level generation to print and play games (for the first time?).
